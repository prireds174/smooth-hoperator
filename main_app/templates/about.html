About the Hoperator

♪Queue the Funky tunes♫


"Smooth Hoperator" is the brain-child of a mujer who likes to drink, but also enjoys the art of craft beer.

One day when she could no longer fulfill the idea of her original app idea, she thought to herself.....'I could use a beer'. As she took a refreshing sip and the delectable hoppy liquid immersed her into a state of relaxtion....an idea was born!


She wanted to save this new bodacios beer and have a record kept of all the beers she would one day consume. Other than keep it in her notes to be forever lost in the nonsense notes she kept


'Smooth Hoperator' was born and became an idea worth sharing to other bodacios beer drinkers. She added in a few additional features to better appease the 'brew' drinkers and what we have today is the idea in its emerging form.
